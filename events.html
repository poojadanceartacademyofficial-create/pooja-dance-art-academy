Workshops & Special Programs

☀️

Summer Intensive

Every May–June, a 15-day intensive workshop for all age groups. Multi-discipline, beginner to advanced. Certificates provided.

🎨

Art Competitions

Students are regularly guided and enrolled for inter-school and citywide art competitions. Coaching provided by Mrs. Rathi personally.

🏆

Dance Competitions

Performance training for local, state, and national level dance competitions. Choreography and costume guidance included.

💻

Online Workshops

Live Zoom sessions for students across India and abroad. Available for dance, painting, singing, and handwriting improvement.

🌟

Cultural Festivals

PDAA students perform at major cultural festivals, school events, corporate gatherings, and community celebrations across Nagpur.

🎤

Felicitation Programs

Annual ceremony honouring students who've completed milestones, won competitions, or shown exceptional dedication. Parents warmly invited.
