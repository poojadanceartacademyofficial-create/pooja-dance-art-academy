Meet the Founder

Mrs. Pooja Rathi

Mrs. Pooja Rathi is the visionary founder and principal instructor of Pooja Dance & Art Academy. With over 40 years of dedicated teaching experience and a lifelong passion for the performing and visual arts, she has shaped the creative journeys of more than 25,000 students across Nagpur — and, students across the globe through online classes.

She founded the academy in 2002 with a single, powerful belief: that every child carries a unique creative spark that deserves to be nurtured with discipline, love, and genuine expertise. Under her leadership, the academy has grown from a small local studio into one of Nagpur's most trusted creative learning institutions.

Beyond teaching, Mrs. Pooja Rathi has built a team of certified, skilled instructors who share her commitment to quality — ensuring every student receives the personal attention they deserve.

Our Journey

Key milestones that have defined over two decades of creative excellence.

  • 2002

    Pooja Dance & Art Academy founded by Mrs. Pooja Rathi in Nagpur, Maharashtra. First batch of dance students begins their journey.

  • 2011

    First Annual Dance Showcase organized — a tradition of excellence that continues every single year to this day.

  • 2015

    Expanded to include multiple creative disciplines — art, singing, acting, instruments, and more. A true creative hub established.

  • 2018

    Crossed 9 creative disciplines under one roof. Students begin winning regional and city-level competitions consistently.

  • 2020

    Launched online classes — bringing the academy's expertise to students across India and globally, uninterrupted.

  • 2024

    25,000+ students trained milestone reached. Achieved 4.7★ on Google and 5-Star certification on JustDial.

  • 2026

    Official website launched. Academy expands its digital presence, reaching students across the globe.
