Our Journey

Key milestones that have shaped Pooja Dance & Art Academy into what it is today.

  • 20XX

    Academy founded with the first batch of dance students. Began with classical dance and quickly expanded to arts.

  • 20XX

    Expanded to include singing, acting, and musical instruments. First Annual Show performed to a packed audience.

  • 20XX

    Crossed 200 enrolled students. Introduced fitness sessions for ladies and shlok learning for children.

  • 20XX

    Began college event collaborations — providing choreography and performance support for college fests.

  • 20XX

    500+ students trained. Launched handwriting improvement and expanded to 9 creative disciplines.

  • 2026

    Launching official website and digital presence to serve the growing community of students across Nagpur.
