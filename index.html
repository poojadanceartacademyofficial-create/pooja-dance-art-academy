About the Academy

23 Years of Shaping Creative Lives

Founded in 2002 by Mrs. Pooja Rathi, Pooja Dance & Art Academy has spent over two decades nurturing the artistic talents of more than 25,000 students in Nagpur — and now, through online classes, students across the globe.

With 35+ years of teaching experience, our founder brings an unmatched depth of expertise across dance, music, art, and cultural education. The academy has held annual showcase events every year since 2011.
