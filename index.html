About the Academy

25 Years of Shaping Creative Lives

Founded in 2005 by Mrs. Pooja Rathi, Pooja Dance & Art Academy has spent over two decades nurturing the artistic talents of more than 25,000 students in Nagpur — and now, through online classes, students across the globe.

With 35+ years of teaching experience, our founder brings an unmatched depth of expertise across dance, music, art, and cultural education. The academy has held annual showcase events every year since 2011, and our students continue to win competitions and perform on prestigious stages.
