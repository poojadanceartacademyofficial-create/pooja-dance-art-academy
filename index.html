About the Academy

A Decade of Nurturing Creative Souls

Pooja Dance & Art Academy was founded with one simple belief — every child carries a spark of creativity that deserves to be nurtured. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted creative learning home for hundreds of students across Nagpur.

Led by our founder with a lifelong passion for the arts, our academy combines traditional discipline with modern methods to create a learning environment that is joyful, purposeful, and deeply enriching.

Our Classes & Programmes

Nine disciplines under one roof — crafted for kids, ladies, and young adults who want to explore their creative potential.

🩰

Dance Classes

Classical, Bollywood, and contemporary dance for all age groups and skill levels.

💪

Fitness Sessions

Dance-based fitness programmes designed especially for ladies and youth.

🎨

Drawing & Painting

Structured art courses from beginner to advanced, including sketching and canvas painting.

🎭

Acting

Stage and screen acting workshops that build confidence, expression, and stage presence.

🎤

Singing

Vocal training in classical and light music, from beginners to advanced students.

🎸

Musical Instruments

Keyboard, tabla, guitar, and more — learn the language of music.

✍️

Handwriting Improvement

Calligraphy and penmanship courses for school-age children to develop beautiful writing.

🕉️

Shlok Learning

Sanskrit shlok recitation — a beautiful bridge to cultural heritage and spiritual grounding.

🎪

College Event Performances

Professional choreography, direction, and performance support for college events and fests.
